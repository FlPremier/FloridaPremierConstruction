ABOUT US 

Welcome to Florida Premier Construction CO. 

Florida Premier Construction Company was founded to provide interior and exterior improvements to your residential & commercial properties. Our services include Roofing,  Interior & Exterior painting, full service gutter replacement, and so much more. If you are interested in any of these services please click here. 

Our Mission

Florida Premier Construction Company mission is to provide the highest-quality workmanship possible. We succeed at this because of the integrity of our subcontractors and staff, our commitment to a solid work ethic, and our passion for staying current with the newest innovations of our industry, with consideration for the environment.

Our Vision

To design, build and deliver, high-quality and cost-effective construction projects on schedule for our clients while providing quality employment and career growth opportunities for Petro-Construction team members.
